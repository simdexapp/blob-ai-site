Questions you'd ask Versa first

Is this actually 18+?

Yes. versaAI is for adults only. The personas use strong language, deal with mature themes, and are not appropriate for minors. We age-gate the site and require age confirmation in-app.

Will Versa do anything I ask?

Almost. Versa will roast, flirt, swear, and play characters. Versa won't help you target real people, generate content involving minors, plan violence, or do anything illegal. The "unhinged" is for vibes, not harm.

Is Versa a real person?

No. Every persona is a fictional character, voiced by a model, depicted as 18+. None are based on, or impersonating, real people.

What models does it run on?

A mix. We route between frontier models (Claude, GPT, Grok) and our own fine-tunes depending on the persona, the task, and what each persona is allowed to say.

How is this different from Rudy / Talkie / Replika?

Rudy is a single character. Replika is a friend/partner sim. versaAI is a multi-persona platform built around personality, voice, and memory — with stronger writing, faster voice, and better tools for making the AI yours.
